Understanding Poverty

Poverty is multi-dimensional. It may involve financial hardship, unstable living conditions, limited access to services, and reduced social mobility.

Definition

More than income

Poverty includes unmet needs related to food, housing, healthcare, education, safety, and dignity.

Impact

Long-term consequences

Economic hardship may contribute to lower educational outcomes, poorer health, and fewer employment opportunities.

Solutions

Support + opportunity

Effective approaches often combine immediate support (basic needs) with long-term empowerment (skills and jobs).

Why SDG 1 Matters

Poverty reduction strengthens families, improves wellbeing, and builds more resilient communities.
